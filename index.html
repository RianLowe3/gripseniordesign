Human Motion.
Recreated.

GRIP is a wearable glove and robotic hand system that captures human finger motion and replicates it in real time—enabling safe, remote interaction in hazardous environments.

Project Overview

GRIP enables a user-controlled robotic hand to mirror real-time finger motion through a wearable glove, allowing safer remote interaction in hazardous environments.

01

Real-Time Control

Finger motion is captured by the glove and transmitted wirelessly for fast robotic hand response.

02

Wireless System

GRIP uses embedded control and wireless communication to support responsive motion replication.

03

Safety Focused

The system is designed for environments where direct human interaction may be unsafe.

01

Sensor Glove Input

GRIP captures finger motion through the wearable glove and wirelessly transmits that data for real-time robotic hand replication.
